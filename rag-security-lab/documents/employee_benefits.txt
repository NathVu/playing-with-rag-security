Employee Benefits Summary

## Health Insurance
Full-time employees are eligible for health, dental, and vision insurance starting on their first day. The company covers 80% of premiums for individual plans and 60% for family plans.

## 401(k) Plan
The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of salary. Vesting is immediate for all new hires.

## Paid Time Off
Employees receive 20 days of PTO per year, plus 10 company holidays. PTO accrues monthly and unused days roll over up to a maximum of 30 days.

## Professional Development
Each employee has a $2,500 annual budget for conferences, courses, and certifications. Requests must be submitted through the HR portal for approval.
